Job Title: 
Java Developer

We are looking for a talented Java Developer to join our team and help design, develop, and maintain high-performance applications. You will work with a team of developers to create scalable, efficient, and secure solutions that meet client requirements.


Responsibilities:


Design, develop, and maintain Java-based applications.

Write clean, efficient, and reusable code.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather and analyze requirements.

Troubleshoot, debug, and optimize existing applications.

Implement software solutions following best practices and coding standards.

Work with databases (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL) and APIs for integration.

Perform unit testing and ensure the quality of the code.

Stay updated on Java technologies and industry trends.
Qualifications:


Proven experience as a Java Developer or similar role.

Strong knowledge of Java, object-oriented programming (OOP), and design patterns.

Experience with Java frameworks (Spring, Hibernate).

Familiarity with databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL) and SQL.

Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.

Knowledge of version control systems (e.g., Git).

Good communication skills and ability to work in a team.
Experience with Agile methodologies is a plus.

Education:


Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent experience).
