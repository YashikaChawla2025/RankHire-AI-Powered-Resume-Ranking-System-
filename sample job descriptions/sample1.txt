Job Title: 
Web Developer

We are seeking a skilled Web Developer to design, develop, and maintain responsive websites and web applications. You will collaborate with designers and developers to ensure high-quality, user-friendly products.


Responsibilities:


Develop and maintain responsive, cross-browser websites and apps.

Collaborate with UX/UI designers on design and user interface.

Write clean, efficient code using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks (React, Angular, Vue.js).

Integrate front-end with back-end and databases.

Optimize web pages for performance, accessibility, and SEO.

Troubleshoot and debug issues.

Qualifications:


Proven experience in web development.

Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks (React, Angular, Vue.js).

Familiar with back-end technologies (Node.js, PHP, Python) and databases (MySQL, MongoDB).

Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Knowledge of responsive design principles and mobile-first development.

Good communication skills and ability to work independently or in a team.

Experience with version control (Git).

Preferred Skills:


CMS experience (WordPress, Shopify).

Cloud platform knowledge (AWS, Azure).

Web security understanding.

Agile development experience.

Education:


Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent experience).
